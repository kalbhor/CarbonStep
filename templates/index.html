CarbonSteps

CarbonSteps helps you track your carbon footprint.
It calculates this using your Google Maps data, and some machine learning magic.
We use Cognitive Services to recognise your car, then use an external API to find out its CO2 emissions.
You can start by uploading your takout.zip file.
